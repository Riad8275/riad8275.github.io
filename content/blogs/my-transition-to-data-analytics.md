For more than 25 years, I have worked in business operations, administration, sales, customer service, human     resources, and management across different industries. These experiences helped me develop strong leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills while supporting organizational growth and operational excellence.

As organizations increasingly rely on data-driven decision-making, I became interested in learning how data can be used to improve performance and support strategic planning. This inspired me to pursue further education in Canada and begin my transition into the field of data analytics.

I am currently completing a Data Analyst Diploma at TriOS College, where I am developing practical skills in SQL, Python, Power BI, Excel, data visualization, and business intelligence. Through hands-on projects and real-world datasets, I have learned how to collect, clean, analyze, and present data to support business decisions.

My business background gives me a unique perspective as an aspiring data analyst. I understand how organizations operate and how data can be transformed into meaningful insights that help managers make informed decisions.

My goal is to combine my extensive business experience with modern analytical skills to contribute to organizations that value innovation, efficiency, and data-driven success.
